Infrastructure

Library

  • The college has a well established fully computerized library with a large collection of textbooks, reference books, Technical and non-technical journals and magazines, educational DVDs and various departments.
  • The library has also subscribed online national and international journals available.
  • Library has a Wi-Fi facility to download various journals and other information.
  • The library has a separate reading area, reference section book bank area, etc.
  • It also has enriched collection of value added cultural and spiritual books by renowned authors to build up moral and inner strength of students.
  • In order to facilitate all the readers in selecting the reading material of their interest, the access to stacks is open to all.
  • The materials have been classified and categorised based in Dewey Decimal Classification (DDC).
  • Library has a rich collection of 37000 books.
